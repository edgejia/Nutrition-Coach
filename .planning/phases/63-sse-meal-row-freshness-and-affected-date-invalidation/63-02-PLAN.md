<objective>
Remove the today-only realtime publish gates from committed meal mutation routes and publish affected-date envelopes for same-day and historical mutations.

Purpose: REAL-01 requires metadata on mutation-driven `daily_summary` events, and D-44/D-45 require historical affected-date emission instead of suppressing historical mutations.
Output: route-level affected-date publish helpers and integration proof for chat and direct meal mutation paths.
</objective>
