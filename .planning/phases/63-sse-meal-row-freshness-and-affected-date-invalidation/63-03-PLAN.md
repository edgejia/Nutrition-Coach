<objective>
Make `client/src/sse.ts` a strict transport validator for the Phase 63 `daily_summary` envelope.

Purpose: The browser treats SSE frames as untrusted input; strict parsing and silent ignore are the first line of defense before same-day reconcile or historical invalidation can run.
Output: client envelope types, calendar-real date validation, strict `daily_summary` parser with a compile-safe legacy callback bridge, and unit proof for invalid-frame no-op behavior.
</objective>

<decision_coverage>
- D-01, D-02, D-03: Client type and parser accept the strict envelope and pass source/affectedDate downstream.
- D-04: Parser rejects payloads that rely on non-envelope freshness proof; no high-watermark fields are required.
- D-05: Parser requires `summary.date === affectedDate`.
- D-20: `client/src/sse.ts` validates/dispatches only; it does not refetch or orchestrate state.
- D-35, D-36, D-37: Invalid frames are silently ignored with no callback and no debug/user-visible signal.
- D-38: `affectedDate` and `summary.date` must match `YYYY-MM-DD` and pass calendar round-trip validation.
- D-41, D-42: Future valid dates are valid calendar keys and are dispatched to the coordinator; product-scope ignore is not implemented in `sse.ts`.
</decision_coverage>
